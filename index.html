ROLLS

Rolls protect you from injury and help you keep moving. The diagonal shoulder roll is the foundation of parkour safety. Master the Safety Roll before moving to Side and Back rolls.

üí° Key Tips

  • Roll DIAGONALLY across your back: shoulder to opposite hip
  • Tuck your chin and look at your armpit as you roll
  • Keep arms rounded, not straight - protect your shoulder
  • Practice on both sides eventually

JUMPS & LANDINGS

Good landings protect your joints. Learn to land soft and quiet - if you're loud, you're absorbing too much force.

5

Jog into precision landing

üí° Landing Keys

  • Land on balls of feet, not heels
  • Knees bend to absorb (but don't collapse)
  • Arms out for balance
  • Quiet = good. Loud = too much force.

LINKING & FLOW

Flow is about connecting movements smoothly. It's not just WHAT you do, but HOW you connect it all together.

üí° Flow Tips

  • Smooth > Fast: Control matters more than speed
  • Think ahead: Where are you going after this move?
  • Use momentum: Let one move feed into the next
  • Breathe: Holding breath = tension = stiffness
